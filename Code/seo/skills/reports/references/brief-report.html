Writing Notes

Voice
Practical and knowledgeable. Write for a marketing manager who's built dashboards before but wants to do it better. Avoid beginner-level explanations of what a KPI is. The reader knows marketing — they need dashboard-specific guidance.
Differentiation Angle
Every current top result is either a vendor product pitch (DataBox, Geckoboard) or a generic content marketing play (HubSpot). None covers the executive communication angle — how to design dashboards that drive decisions, not just display data. Acme's angle: "A dashboard that doesn't change behaviour is a screensaver." Focus on actionable design over comprehensive metric lists.
